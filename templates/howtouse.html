How to Use MediPredict

Follow these simple steps to predict potential medical conditions based on symptoms.

Step 1: Enter Symptoms

Input your symptoms into the provided text box. Use a comma (,) to separate multiple symptoms.

Example: stomach_pain,itching,cough

Step 2: Submit Symptoms

Click the Predict button to analyze your input and generate predictions.

Step 3: View Results

Review the predicted disease(s) along with detailed descriptions, precautions, medications, and more.
